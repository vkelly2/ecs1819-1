My Music Interest

One of my biggest interests is music. I enjoy listening to rap and some other genres. In addition I like creating beats. I use many tools that help me including Soundcloud, FL Studio, and AudioCopy. Soundcloud helps you post your music so the world can hear while FL Studio is a software that you create beats on. AudioCopy is a software that is for making samples which you can put into your music. All these tools make me enjoy one of my favorite hobbies even more than before.
