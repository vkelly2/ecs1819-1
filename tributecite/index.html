Influencers

Charles Hamilton Houston
Charles Hamilton Houston was the Dean of Students at Howard Univeristy, a college Thurgood Marshall attended. Houston would always take time out of his life to help Thrugood strive and advance his law career. He played a major role in Thurgood's life.
NAACP
The NAACP is an organizaion that helped advance the rights of colored people. Thurgood Marshall was recruited by the NAACP to help make laws in the South more equal for colored people. This influenced him because they gave him the resiurces to make his dream possible, getting equal rights.
Thurgood Marshall's Father
Thurgood Marshall's father was a bug influence on his son Thurgood. He would take Thurgood to court meetings on his days off to show him how the legal system worked. This is what lead Thurgood to take a job in this profession.

Thurgood Marshall Background and Awards

Thurgood Marshall was a famous civil rights activist, who currently serves on the Supreme Court. He was a lawyer that helped advance the civil rights for all black people in America. He worked for the NAACP. He also won plenty of court cases including his most famous one " Brown v. Board of Education" which let african Americans go to White Only schools.
